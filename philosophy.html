Our Philosophy

The philosophy of the Polytechnic is reflected in its motto “Technology Towards Development”. This philosophy is being pursued through the provision of courses of instruction, training and research in Technology, Applied Science, Commerce, Management and other fiekds of study that may be approved from time to time by the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE). The NBTE performs this function in its role of monitoring and accrediting programmes mounted in the Polytechnic. This way, the Polytechnic’s Programmes are standardized and harmonizsed with the National Policy on Education.

Some of the courses We Offer:

  • Computer Science
  • Computer Engineering
  • Medicine
  • Mass Communication
  • Microbiology
  • Sociology
  • Civil Engineering
  • Statistics
